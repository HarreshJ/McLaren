Lando Norris

Legend

Record Breaker.

Biography

Lando Norris is one of the best drivers to participate in F1. Lando won F3 and helped his teammate (now Mercades driver) George Russel let their F2 team Carlin win the championship and ended up second himself. Lando rapidly ascended into F1 and quickly made a name for himself with McLaren. After 16 podiums, Lando finally managed to get his first win in Miami 2024!
